Biggest UFC Event Of All Time

The biggest UFC event of all time is UFC 229 (06/10/2018). It is famous because of the fight between Conor Mcgregor and Khabib Nurmagomedov. The event sold approximately 2.4 million pay-per-views and went down as UFC's biggest event of all time.
